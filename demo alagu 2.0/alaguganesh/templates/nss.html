🌟 NSS Club - National Service Scheme

Empowering students to serve the community

🎯 Our Mission

To develop the personality of students through community service, and to instill the idea of social welfare and selfless service among the youth.

📌 Objectives

  • Identify the needs and problems of the community
  • Involve students in problem-solving activities
  • Promote national integration and social harmony
  • Encourage leadership and teamwork
  • Foster a sense of social responsibility

✨ Key Activities

🌳 Tree Plantation
🩸 Blood Donation Camps
🧹 Cleanliness Drives
📚 Literacy & Education Support
🌍 Environmental Awareness
🏥 Health Check-up Camps

🏆 Achievements

  • 500+ trees planted in local villages
  • 1000+ units of blood donated
  • 15+ awareness rallies organized
  • Recognized by District Collector in 2024
